Board
  • Each player controls the cavities on their side, as well as the storage on their right
Way of the game
  • Players sow their seeds alternably, starting on their cavities
  • Depending on the last positions the seed was sown, each player can
    • play again
    • keep some of the opponent's seeds
  • The game ends when one of the players lose all of his seeds
Sowing
  • Chose a cavitie on your side
  • Collect all the N seeds from it
  • Place one seed on the N following cavities (The sowing is made on the anti-horary sense)
Keep sowing
  • If the following cavitie is your storage, place a seed on it too
  • If there's seeds remaining, keep sowing on the opponents side
  • If it reaches the opponents storage, do not sow there
  • You can keep sowing on your side if there's seeds remaining
Last seed sown
  • If your last seed was sown in your storage, you can play again
  • If your last seed was sown in one of your empty cavities, transfer to your storage:
    • The seeds from the opposing cavity (opponent's)
    • The last seed you have sown
End
  • The game ends when one of the players can't play (because all of his cavities are empty)
  • The opposing player collects all the seeds from his cavities and transfers them to his storage
  • The player with more seeds on his storage wins
